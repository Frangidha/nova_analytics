Frequently Asked Questions

How do I procure your services? expand_more

We offer flexible engagement models including project-based work, retainer arrangements, and hourly consulting. After our initial consultation, we'll propose the most suitable structure based on your needs. We can work with your procurement team to ensure all requirements are met.

Do you provide NDAs and confidentiality protection? expand_more

Absolutely. We understand the sensitive nature of analytical projects and offer comprehensive NDAs before any detailed discussions. We have experience working with confidential data in regulated industries and can adapt to your security requirements, including secure data transfer protocols and isolated work environments.

What are typical project timelines? expand_more

Project timelines vary based on complexity, data availability, and scope. Simple preprocessing pipelines may take 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive modeling projects typically require 1-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase and maintain transparent communication throughout to ensure deadlines are met.

Can you work with our existing systems and software? expand_more

Yes, we're experienced with most commercial spectroscopy and chemometrics platforms (Unscrambler, SIMCA, PLS_Toolbox, OPUS, etc.) as well as open-source environments (Python, R). We can deliver solutions that integrate with your current infrastructure or recommend the most appropriate tools for your needs.
